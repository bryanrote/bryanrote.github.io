About

Bryan Rote is currently a full-time student at Bard College in Upstate New York, pursuing a double-major in Computer Science and Music Composition & Theory. His admiration of programming and fondness of music have manifested in a keen interest in fields related to video games and digital signal processing (DSP), as well as more traditional roles such as software engineering and audio production. As that kind of overstimulated creative, he yearns to find job experience in the very fields in which he hopes to build an ambitious career.
